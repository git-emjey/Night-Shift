# Night Shift

**Night Shift** is a narrative-driven psychological horror game set during a late-night convenience store shift.  

---

## Story

You play as a night shift worker in a quiet store, expecting nothing more than another uneventful shift.  
As the night progresses, subtle details begin to feel *off* â€” locked doors, strange discoveries, and lingering silence.

What starts as routine slowly unfolds into something deeply unsettling.

---

## Concept

- **Psychological horror over jumpscares**
- **Minimal UI**
- **Story-first pacing**
- **Short but impactful experience**

The game was designed to feel grounded and realistic, relying on sound design, lighting, and timing to create tension.
